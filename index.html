Raigad District Level Subroto Cup Under-14 Football Tournament
Secured the first position and won the Title

Ryan International School, Kharghar had recently won the title of the Raigad District Level Subroto Cup Under-14 Football Tournament, organized by Raigad District Sports Office at Janata Vidyalaya, Khopoli. They won the finals with a 3-0 victory and secured the first position.

The winning side included Abhishek Thote (captain), Chiranji Akhil (goalkeeper), Amit Chintagada, Rushikesh Bhise, Dinesh Kataria, Krutya Shah, Ansh Rai, Rishabh Rai, Rohan Singh, Kshitij Warang, Prayag Bhosle, Sanket Bhavsar, Kaustubh Sharma, Achyut Karnani, Prabhat Verma and Sahil Thakur.

National Level Debate Competiton
Many students won awards of the World Scholars' Cup Debate Competition

Many students from Ryan International School, Kharghar won trophies and medals of the National Level World Scholars' Cup Debate, held in 2019. The theme of this debate was "A World on the Margins". This 1-day debate included various topics from various subjects. The topics included Unsolved Mysteries, On the Edge of Society, Neglected Histories, Enabling Technologies, Louder than Words, and Voices from the Margins.
